Welcome to Sendfriend!

This is a website designed for the (somewhat) obsessive climber -- i.e., those who want to or already do keep extensive notes on their performance and beta for routes they've tried.

Our platform is designed around allowing users to create beta entries for routes. From that low-level data we're able to also keep track of climber's performance over time, across different crags and areas, and in comparison with other climbers. Our platform enables users to share -- or not -- their notes about specific climbs with their friends. Through this aggregate data we can also provide some interesting metrics about routes themselves, how often they're attempted, flashed, and so on.
